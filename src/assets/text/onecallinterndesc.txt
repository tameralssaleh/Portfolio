Over the summer of 2025, I had completed a 10-week long internship for One Call as an "IT Intern" in application development. I participated in daily meetings in the mornings and afternoons, as well as weekly meetings with business analysts. I worked alongside another intern and two mentors where we worked together to build a centralized dashboard for company software that streamlined communications between One Call and other parties. We primarily worked in C# using the .NET framework for ASPX applications. We also heavily worked on SQL procedures for retrieving data and displaying it on the frontend. We also occasionally used AJAX and client-side JavaScript. At the end of the internship, myself as well as another intern had put together a presentation that would demonstrate our work for One Call executives to view.
