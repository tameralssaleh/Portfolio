Space Battles was my own recreation of the popular game "Space Invaders", with some added twists. Space battles features 20 levels, where each 10th level is a boss fight. Each level gets progressively hard, with enemies gaining extra health, damage to player's HP, and increase projectile speed. The game also features a points tracking system, crash log, and randomized pickups that can give more points, heal the player, etc. This project was worked on during the summer of 2024, with more planned updates in the future when I have the time. This was one of my favorite game development projects.
