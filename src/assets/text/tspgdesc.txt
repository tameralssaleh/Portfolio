Tamer's Slow Progression Mod (TSPG) is a Minecraft Java Edition mod for Minecraft 1.21.x. This mod transforms the typical survival experience by lengthening the time it takes to progress through achievements. Progression is slowed by adding extra steps in between crafting, and other modes of progression. Some mechanics in TSPG include not being able to cut trees without an axe, mining some blocks will drop fragmented versions of that block, wood working station for crafting wooden items and blocks, etc. TSPG adds a bit of realism to the survival experience, fitting for those who like to enjoy minecraft with a similar experience as other survival games such as The Forest, Ark, etc. This mod was written in Java and is still actively being updated.
